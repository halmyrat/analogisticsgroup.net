Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2025

Analogistics Group ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit analogisticsgroup.net or contact us for services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a quote or contact form on our website
  • Send us an email or call us directly
  • Engage with us about freight or logistics services

This may include your name, company name, email address, phone number, and shipment details you choose to share.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • Provide logistics and freight services you request
  • Communicate with you about your shipment or account
  • Send service-related SMS notifications (with your consent)
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Carrier partners: Shipment details shared with vetted US carriers solely to fulfill your freight request.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid court order.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may transfer as a business asset.

4. SMS Messaging

We may send SMS messages directly or via authorized third-party service providers through your mobile carrier. By consenting, you acknowledge that your communication service provider acts as your agent to deliver these messages.

Your mobile information will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Opt-in data and consent are strictly confidential and will not be transferred or shared with affiliates or business partners.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy — typically for the duration of our business relationship plus any legally required retention period. To request deletion of your data, contact us and we will do so promptly unless retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 11.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of any such data collection, we will promptly delete the information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. SMS Terms and Conditions

By opting in to receive SMS messages from Analogistics Group, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

Consent for SMS Communication

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages related to our services. We respect your privacy and will never share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information will be used solely to communicate relevant service-related updates.

Types of SMS Communications

Depending on your consent, you may receive SMS messages including, but not limited to:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Follow-up messages
  • Billing inquiries
  • Promotions or special offers (where applicable)

Message Frequency

The number of messages you receive will vary based on the nature of the communication. For example, you may receive up to 15 messages per week concerning appointments, billing, or other service-related matters.

Potential Fees for SMS Messaging

Standard message and data rates apply according to your mobile carrier's plan. These charges may differ for domestic and international messages. Please consult your carrier for details.

Opt-In Methods

You can opt in to receive SMS messages from Analogistics Group through the following channels:

  • Verbal consent during conversations
  • Submission of online forms
  • Completion of paper forms

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any SMS message you receive from us. Alternatively, you may contact us directly at info@analogisticsgroup.com to withdraw your consent.

Help and Support

If you need assistance or have questions about our SMS service, reply "HELP" to any SMS message or contact us at info@analogisticsgroup.com.

Additional Options

If you prefer not to receive SMS messages, simply do not check the SMS consent box on any forms or decline to opt in.

Standard Messaging Disclosures

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You may opt out at any time by texting "STOP."
  • For help, text "HELP" or visit our Privacy Policy.
  • Message frequency may vary depending on the service provided.
